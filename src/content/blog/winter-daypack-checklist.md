If you only change one habit for winter walking, make it this: pack for delayed return, not optimistic finish time.

## Core layers

- Synthetic or wool base layer
- Warm mid layer
- Waterproof shell
- Gloves and hat

## Navigation and safety

- Offline map on phone
- Paper backup map if possible
- Power bank and head torch
- Emergency contact plan
