This is a practical route for shorter days, with mostly clear paths and regular landmarks through fields and village lanes.

## Route notes

The walk follows sections of the River Wharfe, crosses low pasture, and returns via minor roads and signed rights of way.

## Terrain and conditions

- Mixed tracks and grass paths
- Few steep sections
- Sections may be muddy in winter
